About

This is an application designed to view data about the HHS Unaccompanied Children Program.

About the Data

  • This data represents unaccompanied children who are taken into custody by Customs and Border Protection brought to a facility and processed for transfer to the Department of Health and Human Services (HHS) as required by law.
  • HHS: Department of Health and Human Services
  • HHS holds the child for testing and quarantine, and shelters the child until the child is placed with a sponsor here in the United States.
